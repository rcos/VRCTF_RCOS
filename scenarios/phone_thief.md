# Scenario 3: Phone Thief

## Difficulty: Easy

## Environment
An airport hallway with public chairs and tables. Bags, food, drinks, notebooks, and a keycard are scattered across the floor, seats, and tables.
There is a locked door in the wall with a keycard scanner next to it.

## Task

Get into the employees-only room by picking up the keycard to get access.

## User Prompt

Find a way into the employee room. What do you need to get in?


## 
- **Employee's Keycard**: Can be inspected and put in inventory.
- **Backpack**: Can be inspected.
- **Notebook**: Can be inspected and put in inventory.
- **Soda Bottle** Can be inspected and put in inventory.
  
## Solution
1) Pick up keycard located on top of a table.
2) Interact with door scanner.
3) Select keycard from inventory.
4) Open the door.
